High bar for promotion
Promote to full_ordinance_text only when the issue looks like a broad zoning ordinance publication for the jurisdiction as a whole.

To promote, you should usually be able to point to ALL of the following:
- publication framing such as zoning ordinance or zoning code title,
- broad structure such as multiple articles or sections,
- district or map framework,
- more than one substantive component such as uses plus dimensions plus administration or enforcement,
- no better narrower explanation.

Narrower explanations that defeat promotion
If any of these better describes the issue, keep it out of full_ordinance_text:
- code excerpt or installment only,
- amendment,
- limited-scope ordinance,
- rezoning/map change,
- variance/special-use order,
- notice or summary only,
- narrative or quoted snippets,
- non-zoning ordinance.

Special caution on false fulls
Do NOT promote just because the issue is:
- long,
- formal,
- enacted,
- printed as legal notice,
- full of numbered sections.

Those features are not enough by themselves because specialized regimes amendments and non-zoning ordinances can also look that way.

Frequent false-promotion traps
- sign ordinances,
- flood control or flood hazard ordinances,
- airport zoning,
- subdivision regulations,
- fire or building code amendments,
- mobile home or trailer regulations,
- site-specific rezoning ordinances,
- hearing notices quoting ordinance sections,
- summaries that say they are summaries or not complete.

Allowance for proposed comprehensive ordinances
If the issue truly prints a broad comprehensive zoning ordinance, it may still be full_ordinance_text even if proposed rather than operative.

Other applicable zoning law
Use other_applicable_zoning_law when the issue prints real zoning law but is not a broad comprehensive ordinance publication.

Not applicable
Use not_applicable_printed_zoning_law when the issue does not actually print usable zoning law.

full_ordinance_signals
Set these booleans based only on printed text in the issue:
- official_publication_framing:
  ordinance/code publication framing is explicit
- broad_code_structure:
  multiple sections/articles or similarly broad structured ordinance text are printed
- district_framework_present:
  districts zone map framework or broad zoning framework is printed
- multiple_substantive_components_present:
  at least two of the following appear in printed ordinance text:
  use rules dimensional rules administration/boards enforcement/penalties definitions

Quality flags
Allowed values only:
- mixed_issue_content
- possible_non_zoning_text
- ocr_noise
- comprehensive_but_partial_issue
- broad_publication_but_status_unclear
