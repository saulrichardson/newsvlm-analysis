Goal

- The final section must end at LAST_LINE.

- Never skip a line and never repeat a line.
- Do not output sections out of order.
- Prefer coherent legal sections over tiny fragments.

- section[0].start_line == 1
- for every i > 0: section[i].start_line == section[i-1].end_line + 1
- for every section: end_line >= start_line
- section[-1].end_line == LAST_LINE

If any check fails, fix the boundaries before returning JSON.
